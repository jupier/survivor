LEVEL UP MENU
================================================================================
Title: Level Up!

Options:
- Increase Fire Speed
  Description: Shoot 30% faster

- Increase Projectile Count
  Description: Fire +1 projectile per shot

- Increase Movement Speed
  Description: Move 20% faster

- Increase Targeting Range
  Description: Target enemies 30% farther away

- Activate Slow Weapon
  Description: Unlock slow effect on enemies

- Increase Slow Effect
  Description: Slow enemies by +10% (max 80%)

- Activate AOE Weapon
  Description: Unlock area damage attacks

- Increase AOE Speed
  Description: AOE attacks 20% faster

- Increase Max Health
  Description: Max health +1 and restore health

================================================================================
NOTES
================================================================================
- All strings are case-sensitive
- Some strings contain placeholders like {value}, {status}, etc.
- Format strings should preserve placeholders when translating
- Level names follow the pattern "Level {number}" but can be customized
- Power-up names are displayed with a time remaining suffix (e.g., "Speed Boost: 5.0s")
